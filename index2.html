About Us

“Saving the planet, one chocolate bar at a time.”
—Jane Doe, Co-founder of Coco Chocolatier

Coco Chocolatier started in 2013 as a passion project between Mr. and Mrs. Doe. From the get-go, they wanted to combine their environmental activism with their love of chocolate. When not indulging in bars of chocolate and tending to their backyard chicken coup, you will find the Does travelling to the cacao farms they collaborate with, giving talks on environmental causes around the world, and concocting flavour profiles for new chocolate bars.
